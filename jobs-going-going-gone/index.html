There is no such thing as a job for life any more - The Sydney Morning Herald

Younger workers want to retire at the age of 52

Younger workers in the early years of their career are expecting to retire at the age of 52 - 15 years before the federal government has decided they should stop working. Across all age groups, the expectation of having a job for life no longer exists for more than two thirds of Australian workers, a new national survey has found.
