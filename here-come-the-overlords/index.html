Credit Suisse analyst Eugene Klerk and his team published a note recently on the types of jobs most likely to be "disrupted" by new technology. Most people regard "disrupted" as a euphemism for "destroyed." Klerk cites a 2013 study that found 47% of jobs - mostly in sales, services, and admin - are at risk of being destroyed by new technology or robots.
